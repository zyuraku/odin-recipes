Salt-Grilled & Foil-Grilled King Trout

In the anime, Mukouda prepares King Trout in two ways, one of them wrapped in foil and grilled. Here's a simple take on that.

Ingredients & preps:

  • King trout fillet (or any white fish like snapper)
  • Aluminum foil
  • 1/2, thinly sliced
  • A handful of shimeji and enoki mushrooms
  • 1 tbsp white wine (optional)
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • Ponzu or soy sauce for serving (opt)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven or grill.
  2. Lay a sheet of aluminum foil on a baking tray.
  3. Spread butter on the foil, then layer the sliced onions on top.
  4. Place the trout fillet on the onions, season with salt and pepper.
  5. Add mushrooms and pour in white wine if using.
  6. Fold the foil tightly to seal and bake/grill for 15–20 minutes until the fish is cooked through.
  7. Serve with ponzu or soy sauce on the side.
