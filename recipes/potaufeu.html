In the anime, this dish is served with sausages and bread typical of their fantasy world. Here is a simplified version.

Ingredients & preps:

  • 500g beef (shank or rib)
  • 2 carrots, chopped
  • 2 potatoes, peeled and chopped
  • 1 onion, halved
  • 2 garlic cloves, smashed
  • 1 liter beef broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Sausage (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Boil the beef in water for 5 minutes, then drain.
  2. Bring the beef broth to a boil in a large pot.
  3. Add beef, carrots, potatoes, onion, and garlic to the broth.
  4. Simmer on low heat for 1.5â€“2 hours until beef is tender.
  5. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. If using sausages, add them 15 minutes before the stew is done.
  7. Serve hot with French bread or plain bread.
